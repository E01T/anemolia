More About Anemolia

Idyllically situated on the outskirts of the serene, countryside village of Lefkogeia. Located on a plot of 9000 sqm, complimented by a large olive grove, and a lovely garden with herbs and lawn. A 200sqm, two-storey building with three air-conditioned bedrooms, two of which have ensuite bathrooms on the upper floor. Next to the third bedroom which is downstairs there is a bathroom fully equipped for people with special needs. The bedrooms have got terraces overlooking the pool, the mountains and the bay of Plakias in the distance.

Classic Modern Design

Great layout with quality finishes and a bright and stylish decor. The outdoor space is perfect and the views out across Plakias bay are breathtaking, especially at sunset!

Unique & Creative Hosts

Stella and Manolis are wonderful hosts. Will meet you at the property and have the fridge stocked with all the essentials as well a delicious homemade Moussaka!

Great Location

Remote location on an old olive farm. Provides a peaceful and tranquil setting. Yet close enough to all of the essential amenities and the sea.

Has Everything

Lots of amenities, like free access to WiFi, cable/satellite TV, private parking, private swimming pool, air conditioning, to name but a few.
